## Required

- A doorway pullup bar: the single most consequential upgrade from Tier 0. It closes the pulling gap that bodyweight alone could only approximate. Buy the leverage-mount kind that needs no screws, and test it carefully before you trust it with your full weight
- One heavy kettlebell: 24 kg for most of Path B, 16-20 kg for Path A and smaller-framed trainees. One well-chosen bell runs the entire program. Cast iron is fine; competition steel is nicer and not necessary
- A weight vest: ideally adjustable, 20-30 lb of capacity. This is your ruck load and your loaded-pushup load. A snug pack with soft weights inside works as a substitute
- Real training shoes: flat, stable, and grippy for the kettlebell work; comfortable for long rucks. Avoid soft maximalist running shoes for the strength sessions

## Strongly recommended (under $40 total)

- A pull-up assistance band: a long loop band lets Path A train full-range assisted pullups from week one and lets everyone warm up the pattern. The fastest route to your first strict rep
- A sturdy bench, chair, or low box: for the Turkish get-up, the bent-over row position, and rear-foot-elevated work
- A patch of floor with clearance overhead: kettlebell presses and get-ups need room above you

## The honest gap

Tier 1 has one structural limit: a single bell. With one kettlebell you cannot do true double-bell work (double cleans, double front squats, double presses), and the heaviest hinge loading is capped by what one bell can deliver. We work around this with single-arm ballistics, higher-rep swings, the weight vest, and tempo. It is enough for a year or more of honest progress. When the single-arm work stops challenging you and you want to load the squat and press past what one bell allows, that is the signal for Tier 2: a second bell.

> **A NOTE ON BELL SIZING**
> When in doubt, size down. You can always do more reps with a lighter bell, but you cannot un-strain a back from a bell that was too heavy on day one. The swing is the exception that rewards weight, but only once the hinge is grooved. Earn the heavy bell.
