## C2 - The Strength Hall

A serious strength gym or CrossFit affiliate: full barbells, squat racks, platforms, bumper plates, a complete bell and dumbbell range, rowers and air bikes, and the company of people training hard. The clearest sign you are ready is simple: when you want to load the squat, the deadlift, and the press with a free barbell, past what the leg press and the dumbbells allow, the basic floor has given what it can. C2 walks this same twelve-week arc with the heavier tools.

## Or the home garage

If you would rather train at home, the Tier 3 program (The Real Garage) covers the same barbell-based strength with a rack, a bar, and a cardio machine of your own. The home path runs Tier 0 through Tier 3; C1 and C2 are its commercial-gym siblings. They meet at the same place: a strong, durable, full-spectrum body.

## Or stay here

There is no obligation to move. A basic gym can run this program for years: cycle the dumbbells heavier, the machine pins lower, the rucks longer, and you will keep adapting. For a great many people, a membership and this plan are the whole answer, for the rest of their lives. That is not settling. That is the point.
