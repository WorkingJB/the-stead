> **THE SHAPE OF EVERY WEEK**
> Day 1 strength (push + squat). Day 2 easy aerobic. Day 3 strength (pull + hinge). Day 4 conditioning (Zone 2 in Block 1, tempo in Block 2, intervals in Block 3). Day 5 long ruck. Day 6 optional mobility. Day 7 rest.
