## Strength assessments

Take a full rest day before testing. Each test is one all-out set, full range of motion, controlled tempo. Stop the set the rep before form breaks.

## Aerobic assessments

Run these on separate days from the strength tests and from each other. They are efforts, not races; hold a pace you could sustain.

> **IF YOU HIT YOUR PATH'S STANDARDS**
> You are ready for Tier 1. Buy a doorway pullup bar, a 24 kg (or 16-20 kg for Path A) kettlebell, a weight vest, and proper training shoes. Total cost: roughly $300-500.

> **IF YOU DIDN'T HIT THEM YET**
> Run Block 3 again. Pick the two assessments you fell short on and concentrate volume there. Most people who repeat a block hit standards the second time. The Stead trains people who plan to keep training for forty years; one extra month is not a setback, it's a calibration.
