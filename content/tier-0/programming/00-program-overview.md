> **THE SHAPE OF EVERY WEEK**
> Day 1 strength. Day 2 easy aerobic. Day 3 strength. Day 4 conditioning (Zone 2 in Block 1, tempo in Block 2, intervals in Block 3). Day 5 long Zone 2. Day 6 optional mobility. Day 7 rest.
